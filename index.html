A Year in Chapters

This is a story about two people who met in a study room,
found something rare,
and learned what it means to let go with grace.

It's about growing up,
about the courage to feel deeply,
and the wisdom to walk away when needed.

Written in reflection

Preserved with care

For the me who will look back

Some chapters close quietly. And that's okay.
